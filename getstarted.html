Health Tips for Your Well-being

  • Drink plenty of water daily to stay hydrated.
  • Get at least 30 minutes of physical activity most days of the week.
  • Eat a balanced diet, rich in fruits and vegetables.
  • Ensure you get enough sleep every night (7-9 hours for adults).
  • Manage stress through meditation, yoga, or simple relaxation techniques.
